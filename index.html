About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago, advised by Prof. V. N. Venkatakrishnan. My research interests span cybersecurity, software supply chain security, federated learning, and blockchain-based decentralized systems. Currently, I focus on CI/CD pipeline security and runtime attack detection in GitHub Actions workflows, building systems that combine static analysis with runtime monitoring to detect supply chain attacks that existing tools miss.
